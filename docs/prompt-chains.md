# Prompt Chains

## Chain A
Research → Insights → Script

Step 1:
Extract 5 insights.

Step 2:
Generate 45-second script.

Benefits:
- Reduces hallucination
- Improves factual accuracy

---

## Chain B
Idea → Story Concept → Visual Prompt

Step 1:
Generate 3 concepts.

Step 2:
Convert best concept into image prompt.

Benefits:
- Creative exploration
- Better visual consistency

---

## Chain C
Draft → Compliance → Refinement

Step 1:
Generate draft.

Step 2:
Validate language.

Step 3:
Optimize for audience.

Benefits:
- Enterprise-safe content
- Regulatory compliance
